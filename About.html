I'm a User Interface/Experience/Interaction... designer.

Titles are so awkward. What I do covers more than just one area of experience. I'm primarily a visual designer in the area of interfaces and web experiences. Throughout my career and present position I have also taken on the role of UX and developer and feel comfortable enough in each to communicate and work with a team. I'm not an expert in everything, but I have the ability to deliver a functional prototype from research and UX work all the way to implementation of said prototype.

I'm not every role on every project, not even close. But I feel comfortable taking on any of them for a period of time. Taking on different roles at different times and projects has helped me comprehend every part of the product development process and how to sell it as well.

Design starts at the concept phase, not the visual phase. I understand the ecosystem constanlty strive to learn more. One of my favorite things is when I meet someone who does things better than me or who knows more than me.

This is me
